GATHERING OF THE PIOUS SPOUSE (GPS SERIES IV)

Gathering of the Pious Spouse (GPS) is a singles and married seminar (programme) organized by this noble society to guide and elighten both youths and the married on important marital issues.

This event is geared towards the building a stable and successful islamic home for the benefit of the entire ummah. Coming up soon is the series IV of this event as part of the 1434 Jihad Week programmes.

Date: Saturday 20th Sha'ban (29th June, 2013)
Venue: Okunuga Hall (Faculty of Law)
Time: 4.00pm
Topic: The Perfect Spouse
