    Usrah

Eid Mubarak!!!

May Allah(S.W.T) forgive us, bless us and shower HIS mercy on us this Eid and days to follow. May HE(S.W.T) preserve our soul to witness more of this in our lifetime.

May HE(S.W.T) grant our good wishes & make it easy for us to serve HIM, May HE(S.W.T) accept our ibadah, overlook our shortcomings, make our lives a good example of a Muslim / Muslimah, our grave a pleasant resting place & Jannah our abode in the hereafter. Amin

NB: We are to keep away from foods & drinks till after our eid prayer inshaaAllah. Let's observe the sunnah.
Coutesy: MSSN-LASU EXCOs .

Etiquettes of Eid:
(1) Take d ritual bath
(2) Put on ur best clothings
(3) Go to the eid ground (preferably by foot)
(4) Recite d takbiraat on your way and while waiting for the Imam
(5) Listen to the sermon after the prayers
(6) Change your route back from the prayer ground (it's d Sunnah of the Prophet(S.A.W)).
(7) Spread the greeting: "Taqabbala Lahu minna wa minkum" Eid Mubarak to you and your family.
