SAFETY HEALTH WEEK

The World Environmental Day (WED) is an annual event that is aimed at beign the biggest and most widely celebrated global day for positive environmental reaction. World Environmental Day activities take place all year round and climax on 5 June every year involving everybody from everywhere.

World Environmental Day is also a day for people from all It is run by the United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP).

The theme for this year's World Environmental Day celebration is "Waste to Wealth. Think, Eat, Save" held on June 5, 2013 (26th Rajab 1434AH) at the LASU Mosque. It is an anti-food waste and food loss campaign that encourages you to reduce your foodprint.

During the course of the event, all muslims on campus have decided to give back to the environment by dedicating to clean the school mosque, every faculty and the whole school at large.
