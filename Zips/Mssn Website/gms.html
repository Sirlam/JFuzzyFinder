GRADUATING MUSLIM STUDENTS (GMS '13)

"The Efficient Precursor: The World Awaits You", this is the theme for the first ever LASU-MSSN Graduating Muslim Students' Dinner/Events slated for August 2013 and of course the theme stands as a general one for the entire event. The tenor of this theme is to prepare the budding graduate for the upsurging task of maintaining relevance in the corporate society, existence as a Muslim professional, his contribution to the Deen and to the Society, sustaining a means of livelihood amidst abject poverty, his survival in the face of daunting economic woes and surmounting employment challenges.

The dinner will In sha Allah hold at the magnificent Civic Centre on the highbrow Victoria Island, Lagos with ambidexterous keynote addresses. The first keynote address speaker, a spritual angle will be delivered by a very distinguished Muslim, an excellent administrator and a reputable scholar who has demonstrated exemplary courage and due diligence in the ensuring unity of the Muslim Ummah in Nigeria, the Executive Secretary, Muslim South-West Nigeria, Rtd. Prof. D.O.S. Noibi. While the second wing of the address will be delivered by a passionate Muslim, a dogged Public servant, a dutiful administrator, an irrepressible voice of self-belief, a scholar of Shariah and Western knowledge who has evidenced a consistent voice in the face of economic slavery and political servitude, the Governor of Central Bank, Mallm Sanusi Lamido Sanusi. Other speakers include Mr. Fola Adeola (Founde, GT Bank), Mallam Nasir El-Rufai (Fmr. FCT Minister) and Nokia's Kola Osinowo as they will also be giving a pep talk on Self employment, Potential in Public Service and Private Business; Do's and Don'ts respectively.

ELIGIBILITY

Every matriculated Muslim Student who is in his/her final year of any course, department or unit is an automatic member of the Graduating Muslim Students' Team. There will be no cause of discrimination of any kind, on grounds neither on modes of dressing, level of faith or closeness to the Mosque or MSSN. The ticket to participate and edge your name in gold is beign a Muslim Finalist and it is therefore open to all and sundry who meet these Criteria.

LEVIES

There are different registration rates with substantial discounts from which members can benefit if we act timeously and as there may be no discretionary rates as contained in the form. Payin in installment is allowed but the option can only be exercised three(3) times between now and it ends in June, 2013. The levies to be paid will suffice for the three mainn objectives and graduating Muslim Students' can attend as long as the have paid their token of N3000. There will be coded tickets for other Muslim Students who have the intention of attending the Dinner at a Rate to be decided by the Dinner Programming and Logistics Team and ratified by the Executive Planning Team. In recognition of our status as students, the economic downturn of the country and other possible commitments, the fees have been categorized as follows:

      Graduating Muslim Students from all faculties are to pay N3,000 only
      Members of the Committee and working Teams are to pay N5,000 only
      Special Category members are to pay N10,000 only

SPONSORSHIP

In appreciation of the daunting task of the programme, sponsorship packages and benefit inherent in them will be available for Companies, firms, enterprises and business organizations who want to associate with us. The varieties of sponsorship package are designed to meet their individual business needs and enhance their marketing opportunites. We organize the uniqueness of each business and have tailored packages that can be further customized to meet specific needs.

      Diamond Sponsorship (Executive Partners): This includes an official full colour page on the "Encyclopedia of Muslims" to be published, 2 banners at prime location at the Divine venue, Company logo on banners and public booklets and programme of events, Recognition as a sponsor and a corporate booth in the main arena. This costs N500,000 only.
      Ruby Sponsorship: This includes a standard booth, 2 banners at prime locations at the Dinner, Half page colour advertisement in the "Encyclopedia of Muslims" to be published, company logo on banners and recognition as sponsors. This costs N3000,000 only.
      Pearl Sponsorship: This includes a standard booth, 1 banner at prime location at the Dinner, a quarter page colour advertisement in the "Encyclopedia of Muslims" to be published and recognition as a sponsor. This cost N150,000 only.
