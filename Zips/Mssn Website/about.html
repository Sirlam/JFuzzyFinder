BRIEF HISTORY OF MSSN

The Muslim Students' Society of Nigeria, MSSN, Lagos State University is as a Branch to MSSN, Area Unit, Lagos. It serves as the meeting point for all Muslim students on LASU campus. The society is virtually one of the largest in the world.

The major objective of the society is to bring together all Muslims on campus and to conscientize them about the deen of Islam. This is been achieved by the various programmes organized by the society.

MISSION & OBJECTIVES

The aims of the society are numerous which includes; to unite all muslims on campus and imbibe the spirit of brotherhood, promote the welfarism of muslims, to enlighten them on the faith in Allah (S.W.T) and the Prophet (S.A.W)is HIS Messenger. To make arabic the lingua franca of muslims and so on...

In order to achieve these aims, MSSN-LASU have set up many programmes and events such as the jihad week, usrah, faculty meeting, sister's circle, GPS and so many others to develop muslims on campus.
