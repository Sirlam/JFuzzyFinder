WELCOME

The Muslim Students' Society of Nigeria, MSSN, Lagos State University is as a Branch to MSSN, Area Unit, Lagos. It serves as the meeting point for all Muslim students on LASU campus. The society is virtually one of the largest in the world.

The major objective of the society is to bring together all Muslims on campus and to conscientize them about the deen of Islam. This is been achieved by the various programmes organized by the society. Read more...
